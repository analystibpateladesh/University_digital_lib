Frequently Asked Questions

You can access all course materials through our digital library portal. Navigate to the "Course Material" section from the main menu and use the filters to find materials for your specific degree program, semester, and course. All materials are available online and can be downloaded in PDF format.
Click on the "+ Contribute" button in the top right corner of any page. This will take you to a Google Form where you can submit your materials. Please ensure you have the necessary permissions to share the content and provide accurate metadata including course name, semester, and material type.
Yes, we have a dedicated section for PYQ (Previous Year Questions) papers. You can find them by navigating to "Exam PYQ papers" from the main menu. Papers are organized by degree program, semester, and course. New papers are added regularly after each examination cycle.
For technical support, please contact us at support@uohyd.ac.in or call +91 40 2313 4003 during office hours (Mon-Fri: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM). You can also use the contact form on this page to describe your issue, and our technical team will respond within 24 hours.
Announcements are updated daily. Important notices from the administration, academic departments, and student affairs are posted as soon as they are available. We recommend checking the announcements section regularly or following our social media channels for instant updates.
